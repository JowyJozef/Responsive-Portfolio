About

For as long as I can remember, I've always been a creator. I've always been someone who loves to tinker, modify, create and build things. I've always had this desire to keep learning no matter how difficult the topic may be.

I love the thrill of building things with technology. I've always had a thing for computers and how they work. I love to make music, develop web pages and also see how other web pages work. I hope to one day dig in to machine learning, artificial inteligence and build things from scratch.

I believe it is in me to create, to develop, to problem solve and to understand how things work. And I love doing all of those things with technology.
